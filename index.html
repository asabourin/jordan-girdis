About me

Born in Sydney, Australia, I grew up sailing on windsurfers, dinghies and bigger yachts. Enjoying speed and challenges, I started racing in regattas all over the East coast of Australia.

I discovered kitesurfing in 2009 and immediately got hooked to this exhilarating sport. As kiteracing took off in the last couple of years, I have found myself spending more and more time on my raceboard trying to go as fast as possible. I have been competing in various kite racing events around the world and recently started riding on hydrofoil as well.
